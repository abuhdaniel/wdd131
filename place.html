About Nigeria

Nigeria is a country in West Africa known for its diverse culture, large population, and rich natural resources. It is home to many ethnic groups and languages.

Culture

Nigeria has a vibrant culture with music, food, and festivals. Popular dishes include jollof rice, and Afrobeats music is globally recognized.
