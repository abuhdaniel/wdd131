BuildRight Construction - Website Planning Document

Site Name

BuildRight Construction

This name reflects a professional construction service focused on quality, reliability, and doing the job the right way. It is simple, memorable, and clearly communicates the purpose of the business.

Site Purpose

The website will provide information about construction services including residential building, renovations, and project consultations. It will also showcase past projects, provide contact information, and allow clients to request quotes.

Scenarios

  • How can I request a quote for building or renovating my house?
  • What types of construction services does BuildRight Construction offer?

Color Schema

#2c3e50 – Used for headers and navigation background.

#e67e22 – Used for accents, buttons, and highlights.

#f4f4f4 – Used as the page background color.

Typography

Roboto – Used for body text for clarity and readability.

Merriweather – Used for headings to give a strong and professional appearance.
