Website Planning Document

Site Name

BuildRight Construction

This name represents a professional construction company focused on delivering quality building and renovation services done the right way.

Site Purpose

The website will provide information about construction services such as residential building, renovations, and consultations. It will also showcase projects and allow users to request quotes.

Scenarios

  • How can I request a quote for building or renovating my home?
  • What types of construction services are available?

Color Schema

#2c3e50 – Used for headers and navigation.

#e67e22 – Used for highlights and accents.

#f4f4f4 – Used for page background.

Typography

Roboto – Used for body text.

Merriweather – Used for headings.
